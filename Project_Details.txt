Project Overview:
Climate change, rising carbon emissions, and unsustainable resource consumption have become some of the world's most critical challenges. 
EcoHub AI is an intelligent sustainability platform that leverages Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Data Analytics to help 
individuals understand, monitor, and reduce their environmental impact.

The platform analyzes real-world lifestyle factors including transportation habits, electricity consumption, water usage, dietary patterns,
waste generation, plastic consumption, renewable energy adoption, internet usage, and household characteristics to accurately predict an 
individual's carbon footprint. Beyond prediction, EcoHub AI evaluates sustainability behavior through a Green Score, identifies user
segments using Clustering, classifies emission levels through Machine Learning, and generates AI-powered personalized recommendations to 
encourage environmentally responsible decisions.

The project integrates Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Explainable AI, Interactive Dashboards, and Generative AI to transform raw
environmental data into actionable sustainability insights. By combining intelligent prediction with personalized guidance, EcoHub AI 
empowers users to adopt greener lifestyles while contributing to global climate action.

Expected Outcome:
Develop an intelligent AI-driven sustainability platform capable of predicting carbon footprint, evaluating environmental behavior,
generating personalized eco-friendly recommendations, and enabling data-driven decision-making for individuals and organizations to 
promote sustainable living and reduce carbon emissions.
